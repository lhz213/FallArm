about us

Fall Alarms offers several devices enabling you to find the right solution for you or your loved one

Website Fall Alarm


You can directly help your loved one to send a Fall Alarm to system though a Desktop or a Laptop. Just simply login to website and click the Alarm button.

Mobile Phone Fall Alarm

You can also give a Mobile Phone installed Fall Alarms app and keep login. Then teach your loved one to click the Alarm button himself/herself after fell.

Fall Detectors (Coming Soon...)

A good fall alert system includes fall detectors which can be worn on a cord around your neck, clipped to your belt or worn with a waist pouch accessory.

our services

After receiving the fall alarms, our customer service representative will contact patient and patient's relative immediately. After confirmed the fall, we will send our nursery to patient house do following exam then determine if need send to hospital.
